MNISTprediction

The goal of this project was to design and evaluate three different neural networks to solve the problem of handwritten digit prediction. I used the MNIST benchmark dataset, which contains 60,000 images of handwritten digits and their corresponding labels. I trained the models on the dataset and compared their performance based on accuracy, efficiency, and other relevant metrics to determine which architecture was most effective at recognizing the digits.
